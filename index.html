About

Dear reader. I am a master's student at KTH in Stockholm, Sweden. Currently, I am studying towards an MSc in Interactive Media Technology with focus on Computer Graphics. My main interests withing the field is within animation programming, computer graphics and game development. My interests in animation and computer graphics started when I went on an exchange semester at KAIST in South Korea. At KAIST I took a course in Motion Graphics, where I got to learn a lot in the animation programming field, as well as a course in computer graphics.

TravellAR is a course project in the course Advanced graphics and interaction. TravellAR is a co-op augmented reality experience meant to bring people closer to each other at a distance. The game is best described as a puzzle exploration game, letting the users exlpore the world around them.

Viscous Biscuit is a course project in the course Computer Game Design. Viscous Biscuit is a puzzle platformer game.
