About

I am a master's student at KTH in Stockholm, Sweden, and I am currently doing my masters thesis in animation programming at Avalanche Studios. At KTH I am studying towards an MSc in Interactive Media Technology with focus on Computer Graphics. My main interests withing the field is within animation programming, computer graphics and game development. My interests in animation and computer graphics started when I went on an exchange semester at KAIST in South Korea. At KAIST I took a course in Motion Graphics, where I got to learn a lot in the animation programming field, as well as a course in computer graphics.
